### Owner (DENT001)

- User management
- Financial reports
- Payroll approval
- System configuration

### Admin (STAFF001)
- Staff management
- Payroll processing
- Expense tracking
- Reports generation
- Cannot modify owner settings

### Front Desk (STAFF002)
- Patient management
- Appointment scheduling
- Payment processing
- Basic reporting

### Dental Assistants (STAFF003, STAFF004)
- Patient records (assigned patients only)
- Inventory management
- Time tracking
- Own payroll access

### Dentists (DENT002-DENT008)
- Own schedule management
- Assigned patient records
- Performance dashboard
- Own payroll access
- Cannot access other dentists' data
